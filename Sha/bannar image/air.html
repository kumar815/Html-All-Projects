    Product Description
    Durable Air Conditioner

    This AC features anti-corrosive gold fins that make sure that water or dust don’t affect the functioning of the AC. This way, you can enjoy uninterrupted cooling and durable usage.

    Turbo Cool

    Thanks to the turbo cool feature, you can enjoy fast and effective cooling in a matter of minutes, even when the weather outside is extremely hot.

    Hidden Display

    The AC features a hidden light-up display that adds an aesthetic touch to the appliance and your room.

    Self Clean Technology

    This AC makes use of the self clean technology to ensure that there’s no build-up of dust, mold, or moisture inside the indoor unit. Every time the AC is turned off, the appliance operates for a few more minutes to dry off and clean the coil in the indoor unit. This way, you can enjoy clean and fresh air cooling when you switch on the appliance.

    Fuzzy Logic

    With the fuzzy logic feature, the AC ensures that you get to enjoy uninterrupted sleep all through the night without any discomfort.

    Self Diagnosis

    The AC will display an error code when there's a technical issue, ensuring fast and easy diagnosis. This way, you can protect the appliance from irreparable damage.
